UCL Top Trumps

A browser-based Top Trumps prototype for a public history project. Players choose the number of players, flip cards, choose one category, reveal all players’ top cards, and then compare scores.

Choose number of players

This version is capped at 3 players and leaves room for you to replace the placeholder content with your own drawings, biographies and categories.
